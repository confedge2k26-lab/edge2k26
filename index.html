About EDGE

EDGE 2026 is conducted by VIT Bhopal University and is proposed to be published as peer-reviewed proceedings with Springer Nature.

Theme

The event addresses models, methods, and engineered systems enabling intelligent, generative, and context-aware behaviour in digital environments. It connects foundational research in artificial intelligence and machine learning with deployed solutions across healthcare, finance, manufacturing, governance, cybersecurity, education, and related domains.

Objectives

  • To present recent advances in digital and generative intelligence.
  • To provide a platform coordinated by VIT Bhopal University for global collaboration.
  • To facilitate high-quality, peer-reviewed publications with Springer Nature.
  • To encourage responsible and explainable AI practices in research and deployment.

Tracks and Topics

Submissions are invited, but not limited to, the indicative tracks outlined below.

Track 1: Digital Intelligence Foundations

  • Machine learning and deep learning algorithms.
  • Hybrid and neural–symbolic intelligence.
  • Optimization, robustness, and uncertainty modelling.

Track 2: Generative AI and Language Technologies

  • Large language models and transformer-based architectures.
  • Multimodal, multilingual, and low-resource AI systems.
  • Conversational AI, dialogue systems, and speech technologies.

Track 3: Engineering Intelligent Systems

  • Edge AI, distributed and federated learning.
  • MLOps, deployment pipelines, and benchmarking.
  • Data engineering and synthetic data generation.

Track 4: Applications and Responsible AI

  • Sectoral use cases in health, finance, governance, and education.
  • Cybersecurity, anomaly detection, and threat intelligence.
  • Ethics, transparency, governance, and societal impact studies.
